# HeyGen Avatar IV Quick Start Guide

## Create Your First Music Video in 5 Minutes

### Prerequisites
- HeyGen account (free or paid)
- Avatar photo (1024x1024+ resolution)
- Audio file of your song/rap (MP3 or WAV)

---

## Method 1: Photo-to-Video (Fastest)

### Step 1: Access Avatar IV
1. Log into HeyGen
2. Navigate to **Avatar IV** section
3. Click **Create New Video**

### Step 2: Upload Your Avatar
1. Click **Upload Photo**
2. Select your character image
3. Wait for upload confirmation

### Step 3: Add Your Audio
**Option A: Upload Audio File**
1. Click **Upload Audio**
2. Select your song/rap file
3. Confirm upload

**Option B: Use Text + Voice**
1. Type or paste lyrics in **Script** field
2. Select voice from **Voice** dropdown
3. Adjust voice settings (optional)

### Step 4: Customize Motion (Optional)
1. Click **Add Motion Prompt**
2. Enter gesture description:
   ```
   Holds microphone, nods to beat, gestures during chorus
   ```
3. Enable **Enhance Motion** toggle

### Step 5: Generate
1. Choose quality: **Faster** or **Higher Quality**
2. Click **Generate Video**
3. Wait 2-5 minutes for processing

### Step 6: Download
1. Preview your video
2. Click **Download** when satisfied
3. Video saved as MP4

---

## Method 2: Studio (Multi-Scene)

### For Longer or Complex Videos

1. Open **HeyGen Studio**
2. Click **New Project**
3. Select **Avatar IV Engine**
4. Add scenes:
   - Scene 1: Verse 1 (upload audio segment)
   - Scene 2: Chorus (different angle photo)
   - Scene 3: Verse 2 (same avatar, different motion)
5. Add transitions between scenes
6. Generate full video

## Quick Tips

### Best Photo Selection
✅ Clear facial features
✅ Good lighting
✅ Front-facing or slight angle
✅ High resolution (1024x1024+)

❌ Blurry or low-res
❌ Extreme side profile
❌ Dark or heavily shadowed

### Audio Quality Matters
✅ Clear vocal track
✅ Proper mixing (vocals forward)
✅ Professional quality recording
✅ MP3 320kbps or WAV

❌ Muddy or distorted audio
❌ Vocals buried in mix
❌ Low bitrate files

### Motion Prompts
✅ "Gestures with hands, nods head"
✅ "Sways gently, holds microphone"
✅ "Confident stance, points at camera"

❌ "Embodies joy and energy"
❌ "Dances around the stage"
❌ "Complex choreographed routine"

## Common First-Time Issues

### Issue: Lip-sync is off
**Solution**:
- Use higher-quality audio
- Ensure vocals are clear in mix
- Try "Higher Quality" generation mode

### Issue: Motion looks robotic
**Solution**:
- Simplify motion prompt
- Let AI use default motion
- Generate 2-3 versions, pick best

### Issue: Character doesn't look right
**Solution**:
- Use different source photo
- Ensure good lighting on face
- Try multiple photo angles

### Issue: Video is too short
**Solution**:
- Paid plan allows up to 180 seconds
- Break long songs into multiple clips
- Edit together in video editor
